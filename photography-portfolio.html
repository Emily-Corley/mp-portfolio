Photography Portfolio

Architecture Around Manchester

In this project, I have chosen to take a series of photographs to capture the architecture around Manchester. I wanted to capture both modern and traditional buildings as well as experimenting with various photography techniques.

Photograph One

MediaCityUK, Salford

This photograph of the architecture in Media City was taken during sunset. I positioned the camera lower down that you usually would in order to get the symmetrical reflection in the water.

Photograph Two

Deansgate-Castlefield Tram Stop

This photograph was taken from Deansgate-Castlefield tram stop at night, with the four towers of Deansgate Square framed by the arch of a bridge leading to Deansgate Train Station. This is an example of the photography technique frame within a frame

Photograph Three

Manchester Central Library

This is a long exposure photograph of a tram going past Manchester Central Library. I acheived this effect by setting my shutter speed to 1/4 which allows more light to enter the camera and create a motion blur effect.

Photograph Four

Manchester Cathedral

This is a photograph of Manchester Cathedral which was taking from a low angle looking upwards using a wide angle camera lens.

Photograph Five

Media City UK,Salford

This photograph of the BBC building was taken in Media City UK,Salford during golden hour. In this photo I tried to adhere to the rule of thirds to ensure there wasn't too much dead space.
